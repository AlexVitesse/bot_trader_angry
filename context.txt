PROJECT_NAME: Binance Futures Scalper (Python)
TYPE: Algorithmic Trading (Mean Reversion)
TARGET EXCHANGE: Binance Futures
TARGET PAIR: BTC/USDT
CAPITAL: $10 USD (Micro-Account / High Risk Limit)
LEVERAGE: 5x (Dynamic)

STRATEGY: "BB-RSI Mean Reversion"
- Timeframe: 5m or 15m (To avoid extreme noise of 1m)
- Entry Long: Price < Lower BB AND RSI < 30
- Entry Short: Price > Upper BB AND RSI > 70
- Exit: Price crosses Middle BB (SMA 20)

TECH STACK:
- Language: Python 3.10+
- Connectivity: ccxt (Async), Binance Websockets.
- Data Storage: InfluxDB (Real-time ticks) + Parquet (Historical).
- Backtesting: vectorbt o backtrader (Python libraries).

CRITICAL CONSTRAINTS:
1. Minimum Order Size: Binance requires ~$5 minimum notional. With $10 capital, strict position sizing is needed.
2. Risk Tolerance: 100% loss acceptable (Experimental Phase).
3. Latency: Local execution. Acknowledge network delays (150-300ms).
4. No Manual Intervention: Bot must handle Entry, Stop Loss, and Take Profit.
